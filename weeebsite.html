ABOUT MYSA

Mysa serves to make students’ lives easier. With the stress that they face due to school, students often neglect their mental wellbeing. With Mysa, students can track their emotional health and link it to their academic performance.

The goal for Mysa is to provide students an easy, accessible way to organize their life, improve mood, and enhance their general wellbeing. Mysa provides an interactive calendar where the user can log their emotions using color-coding. Using the data logged, Mysa tracks any patterns that have a negative impact on the user’s life and offers solutions to provide the help that they need.

Due to stress that students face because of school can take a toll on their mental health. It's our goal to help balance their life.

Q1: How will you keep track of my mood?

A1: Each day, you will log in and select a color based off of your mood and how your day went.


Q2: Will anything be done with my data? Will anyone else see my personal info?

A2: Our top priority is to protect your privacy. Everything you tell us will only be utilized to give you a solution.


Q3: What type of solutions do you offer?

A3: We offer feedback from an automatic response that helps us gauge your emotions and enable us to come up with a solution. If we see that you have been feeling down for multiple days, we will make sure to give you the necessary assistance.


Q4: What color is associated with what emotion? Can I customize colors?

A4: Of course! We make sure that the user experience is unique to each person and their preferences.


Q1: What do you track under academics?

A1: We will track your GPA, your growth in courses, calculate grade predictions, and what you could do to improve.


Q2: How does the mental health tracker intertwine with the academic tracker?

A2: This corelates with our mental health tracker because we use both to figure out how a dip in one can help the other.


Q3: What do you do to help us improve with our grades?

A3: We will give links to academic sites that will use our specific code to give you discounts and benefits.
